Overview

Purpose

This website will be a web and informational site that will look great, be intuitive, and informative about motorhome rentals through Idaho Recreation LLC. It will include photos of all available motorhomes, amenities, and rates. In addition, this site will consist of links and pictures to guide the renters on how each amenity does and doesn’t work.

Audience

Motorhome renters who are renting from Idaho Recreation LLC

Content

Home page

[Site visitors will ultimately be on a search for critical information regarding their rental. One activity I thought about was potentially having a safety quiz of sorts. This would entail having the visitors watch all the walkthrough/how-to videos. Then, after the videos, they would be required to take a basic quiz to test their knowledge and ensure that they have adequate information for each rental aspect. We have had issues knowing if renters have watched the videos we send to them in the past. This would be a way more accessible way to ensure that the renters have observed them before each rental and reduce the number of questions/issues that come up during rentals. Other activities would include watching the videos themselves, and we could even add a section for them to send an inquiry or any questions over the website.]

Images for the Home page

[The Information Renters Need]

[I must collect much necessary information to ensure it is all on the website. This information would include photos of the motorhomes, written descriptions of each of them and what amenities they offer, tutorials for how to perform basic tasks and functions associated with the motorhome rental, and even previous reviews from other renters that have been left. I would also need to include information like the RV capacities, how long they are, how many people they sleep, and other essential details. I plan to get all this information from the RV share page, which is currently the only public display of each RV we have]

Images for The Information Renters Need

[Why Idaho Recreation LLC?]

[As I mentioned, we have an RVshare site for each RV, where renters go to look for RVs and the site where the booking takes place. RVshare is a company that takes a commission from RV owners and facilitates the financial and organizational aspects of each booking. RVshare is excellent, but it is more about showcasing the RV in a way that makes it appealing to potential renters. The website that I have created serves a bit of a different purpose: to provide information to those who have already confirmed their booking and are preparing for their trip. They will come to this site because it will contain all the specifics and logistics of each rental and what each rental needs to know before their journey starts. ]
